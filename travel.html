Parking

The venue has a main car park, as well as an overflow car park field. Cars must not be left overnight unless guests are staying at Hedsor House. Please note, all cars need to vacate the car park by 10:00am the morning after the event.

Train

Taplow is the nearest train station to the venue (3.6 miles).

From London you can take the underground tube (Elizabethan line) directly to Taplow station, which is 7 minutes by car to the venue.

Flying

Heathrow is the closest airport to the venue.

The Elizabeth Line runs to Taplow (3.6 miles to venue) and provides direct connections to London Heathrow.
